About Steyon

We are a small but excellent development team that specializes in creating free, individually customized Minecraft plugins.

Our goal is to help small Minecraft servers offer their players a unique experience without having to buy expensive premium plugins.

Each plugin we develop is unique and specially tailored to the needs and ideas of our customers. We don't offer "one-size-fits-all" solutions.
